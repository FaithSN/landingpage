The Bands (Sorry 'bout the videos. WGM stuff)

Paramore
One of the most phenomenal rock bands EVER. Hayley Williams has a unique voice that capture the hearts of fans. In my opinion, Paramore has so much fun with their songs and they carry deep messages, plus with their mild kind of rock they are one the bands every person must know.

echosmith
The less-rock version of paramore and the lead singer even sounds like Taylor Swift O.O (at times). It's unimaginable how a group of teenagers could be so famous, but I guess with that kind of skill, it is possible. I like how their songs have a mild rock feel and country element too.

DAUGHTER
You just gotta love that soothing, calm voice paired with the minimalistic background instrumentals. Their songs tend to be slow, which is a good thing, because you could feel all the feels slip under your skin. Perfect voice with the perfect band and the perfect lyrics! A must-listen.

Up Dharma Down
Never heard much OPM yet or craving for it? Well, you should try out Up Dharma Down(UDD). To be honest, OPM is underrated and bands like these need a little more sunshine. UDD really pours in a lot of emotion into their songs and I'd just hate to see all of that go to waste. If you've got the time, try catching them at the UP Fair, they're usually there.

All these games and you still chose to play my heart...

League Of Legends

League of Legends is one of the few MOBA (Multiplayer Online Battle Arenas) which are famous. MOBA's first came from a Warcraft 3 mod named DOTA(Defense of the Ancients). It's different from the others because it has a greater balance, a more user-friendly HUD, a division-rank system, and easy-to-distinguish graphics.

Clash of Clans

Initially I disliked CoC, because it takes too long for upgrades and stuff like that, but after trying it and getting to th6, I began to enjoy it, because that's where all the fun is. Now, I appreciate the long waiting time because things get busy during the day, and for me, Clash of Clans is a game you can play to relieve stress. (but you should remember the war schedule!)

Magicka: Wizard Wars

Wizard Wars is one of the best PvP games, in my opinion. With a very fast-paced gameplay and hundreds of skill combinations to choose from, that really tests your reaction time because of the spells and counterspells, you can choose from three different game modes that lasts 15-30 mins only. It's a fun game to play when you're pressed for time or just want to relax for a bit. The reward system is also good, and your level does not have a major significance on your performance in the battlefield, it's all based on mastery alone.
